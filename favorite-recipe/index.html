Pearl Cous Cous Fried Rice

Fast, clean, fried-rice style cous cous using leftovers and vegetable scraps. Great hot or cold and keeps well in the fridge all week.

Ingredients

  • 4 Cups Water
  • 2 Cups Pearl Cous Cous
  • 2-3 bullion cubes of your choice
  • 4 eggs
  • any left over cooked meat or none at all to keep it vegetarian
  • any frozen thawed out peas, carrots, thin green beans
  • green onion
  • cilantro
  • siracha to taste
  • 3 teaspoons of sesame oil
  • honey and/or sweet and sour sauce to taste
  • teryiaki and/or soy soyce to taste

Directions

  1. Bring water to a rolling boil then add in cous cous and boullion. Cook 5 minutes and then cover it, set aside and let it finish cooking without any heat on it
  2. Scramble eggs
  3. chop meat and any vegetables
  4. Make sure all ingredients are cool and mix with a rubber spatula in a large bowl
  5. Add in all sauces and oil to taste, do what tastes good and dont worry about being technical
  6. Eat cold, store in the fridge as is, or heat up a pain with oil in it
  7. Fry everything until it starts to crips on the bottom and kill the heat
  8. garnish with sweet n sour and cilantro
