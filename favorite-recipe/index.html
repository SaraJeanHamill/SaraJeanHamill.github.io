Pearl Cous Cous Fried Rice

Fast, clean, fried-rice using leftovers and vegetable scraps. Great hot or cold and keeps well in the fridge all week.

Ingredients

  • 4 Cups Water
  • 2 Cups Pearl Cous Cous
  • 2-3 bullion cubes of your choice
  • 4 eggs
  • any left over cooked meat or none at all to keep it vegetarian
  • any frozen thawed out peas, carrots, thin green beans
    • green onion cilantro siracha to taste 3 teaspoons of sesame oil honey and/or sweet and sour sauce to taste terriaki and/or soy soyce to taste
